Bubble Game

Explanation of how the game works...
In this game, a random number will be generated and displayed as the 'hit number'. You will see bubbles with different numbers on them. Your goal is to click on the bubble that has the same number as the hit number. If you click the correct bubble, your score will increase by 10 points. Try to click as many correct bubbles as you can within the given time limit to maximize your score.
